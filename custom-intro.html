Cryptode is a command-line-driven OpenVPN client for macOS (Sierra, High Sierra, Mojave) with two focus areas:

  • CLI usage for automation.
  • Security for protection of your OpenVPN private keys and certificates, and ensuring that only approved routes are added to your route table.

RHEL/CentOS and Ubuntu support are planned.

About Cryptode

In Greek, "crypt-" means hidden, and "-ode" means path.

Cryptode is intended to protect you using a hidden path.
